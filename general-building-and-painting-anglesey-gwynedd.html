Brick and blockwork by experienced local builders

From garden walls to small extensions, our bricklayers deliver straight, level courses and clean joints. We handle footings, damp courses and lintels with care so the job lasts in coastal weather across Anglesey, Gwynedd and North Wales.

  • Small extensions and internal wall alterations
  • Concrete pads, steps and retaining walls
  • New openings with lintels and tidy making good
  • Match existing brickwork and pointing style

How we build

  • Survey - check access, ground and drainage.
  • Set out - accurate lines and levels.
  • Build - correct mixes and neat joints.
  • Finish - clean down and proper protection.
Can you source matching bricks?
Yes. We supply close matches and trial blends so the new work ties in with the property.

Repointing and structural repairs that protect your home

We repoint brick and stone with the right mortar for the substrate, then tackle minor structural issues like cracked sills or loose coping. Professional repointing keeps water out and lifts the look of walls around Bangor, Holyhead, Menai Bridge, Llandudno and Conwy.

  • Lime and cement mortars to suit the building
  • Stitching cracks and replacing damaged bricks
  • Chimney maintenance and weather details
  • Careful clean down that protects surrounding surfaces

  • Colour advice and sample patches
  • Dust control and full sheet protection
